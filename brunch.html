The Course Menu

A Journey Through Flavor

A thoughtfully curated two-course experience — one appetizer and one entrée — designed to be savored slowly, with bottomless mimosas or house sangria to match.

First Course

Appetizers — Your Choice of One

  • KamiKoi Leche de Tigre

    Fresh fish marinated in leche de tigre, topped with yellow pepper sauce and fried fish.

  • Nikkei Causa Trio Brunch

    A refined trio of Peruvian causas with crab, shrimp and chicken, bright citrus and fresh herb accents.

  • Chicken Tostones

    2 Hawaiian tostones with chicken salad, topped with avocado and yellow pepper sauce.

  • Tostones KamiKoi

    Hawaiian tostones with crab salad, topped with avocado rocoto sauce and yellow pepper sauce.

  • Tropical Roll

    Tempura shrimp, avocado, cream cheese and scallions, topped with sweet plantain and spicy mayo.

  • Dinamita Roll

    Krab salad inside, topped with tobiko, sesame seeds and eel sauce.

  • KAMI Sunrise Roll

    Sushi rice with chives, omelette and cream cheese, topped with fresh salmon and avocado.

Main Course

Entrées — Your Choice of One

  • KamiKoi Chicken Saltado Brunch

    Wok-seared chicken in a bold soy reduction with sautéed vegetables over crispy tostones.

  • Crispy Chicken Scramble Eggs

    Golden crispy chicken over fluffy scrambled eggs folded with sautéed vegetables and fresh herbs.

  • Crispy Chicken Picarón Benedict Waffle

    Artisanal waffle, crispy chicken, poached egg and picarón honey with a sweet-savory balance.

  • Very Berry Waffle

    Waffle served with fresh fruit and a house-made syrup.

  • Tuna Acevichado Benedict Waffle

    Artisanal waffle layered with tuna tartare, poached egg and vibrant acevichado sauce.

  • Salmon Tartar Acevichado

    Artisanal waffle with salmon tartare in delicate leche de tigre, avocado and chili notes.
