Our Story

Experience the best of two worlds — Japanese craft and Latin heart.

Who We Are

A Fusion of Heritage & Craft

KamiKoi Sushi Fusion was born from a simple belief: that the precision of Japanese cuisine and the warmth of Latin flavor belong together. Every plate is a meeting of two cultures — delicate, bold, and unmistakably Miami.

From Nikkei ceviches and chef-driven sushi rolls to wok-fired rice and premium grilled cuts, our menu is designed to surprise and satisfy.

Our Promise

Fresh, Natural, Made for You

We work only with carefully selected ingredients and keep our flavors 100% natural — no additives. Each dish is prepared to order so every visit tastes its best.
